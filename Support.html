Xtreme Motherboards

Make sure that the power cables are properly connected.
Ensure BIOS versions are compatible with installed CPU. BIOS flashback recommended.
CPU Thermal Application is poor. Please replace thermal compoound and try again.
Faulty components may lead to improper startup.
Physical/Electrical damage. Motherboard falling or an electrical failure can brick your motherboard. Please contact support for further help.
Faulty Product from factory.

Hi-Res Displays

Try reinserting or replacing power cord with another power cable.
Verify the monitor has power by looking for a blue, green, or orange light which can be found on the front or the bottom of the monitor's bezel.
If you can view your monitor's settings menu even though the screen is blank, you likely need to turn up the brightness and contrast.
If you have followed the recommendations above and still no picture appears, your computer may have either a bad monitor, video card (graphics card), or motherboard. Please contact support.

Thermal Velocity Products

Very often if your water cooler is not working, the problem can be traced to the power supply. So as a first step, check that it is properly plugged in at the wall and the mains power is turned on. If it is switched on but not dispensing water, the next step is to check your water supply. Ensure adequate water is filled.
Faulty pump may lead to no water flow.
Improper thermal paste application may lead to worse performance, try reinstalling thermal greese.
Ensure your water loop is dust free and water is clean of any bacteria.
