Digital TVC-E5 Air Cooler
Tailored to provide superior RAM and PCIe compatibility, the TVC-E5 is an asymmetrical single fan version of Digital flagship model, the TVC-E5. Thanks to its asymmetrical design, the TVC-E5 clears the top PCIe slot on most µATX and ATX motherboards. At the same time, the single fan setup and recessed lower fins guarantee 100% RAM compatibility with memory modules of up to 65mm height. Users who have sufficient room can also upgrade the cooler with either a 120mm or round 140mm fan on the front fin stack for further improved performance in dual fan mode. Topped off with the trusted, pro-grade SecuFirm2™ multi-socket mounting system, Digital's proven D2-F5 thermal compound and full 15 years manufacturer’s warranty, the TVC-E5 forms a complete premium quality solution that combines dual tower efficiency with excellent compatibility and flexible upgrade options.
